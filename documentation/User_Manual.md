# User Manual

Welcome to InvestIQ AI! This manual will guide you through the features of your AI-Driven Financial Portfolio Advisor.

## 1. Getting Started
### 1.1 Registration
1. Navigate to the Sign Up page.
2. Enter your Name, Email, and a secure Password.
3. Select your Risk Profile (Conservative, Moderate, or Aggressive) so the AI can tailor its advice.

### 1.2 Login
1. Navigate to the Login page.
2. Enter your credentials. If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" to receive a reset link via email.

## 2. Dashboard Overview
Upon login, you will see your main dashboard. It provides:
*   Total Net Worth across all portfolios.
*   Market trends and recent news.
*   Quick actions to chat with the AI Advisor.

## 3. Managing Portfolios
### 3.1 Creating a Portfolio
1. Click "Portfolios" in the sidebar.
2. Click "Create New Portfolio".
3. Provide a name (e.g., "Retirement Fund").

### 3.2 Adding Assets
1. Open a portfolio.
2. Click "Add Asset" and enter the stock symbol (e.g., AAPL, RELIANCE), quantity, and purchase price.

## 4. Using the AI Advisor
1. Navigate to the AI Chat screen.
2. Type questions like "Analyze my Retirement Fund" or "Should I buy more Tech stocks given my moderate risk profile?"
3. The AI will evaluate your current holdings and provide personalized, actionable financial advice.
